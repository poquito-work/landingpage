### UI/UX Design Direction: Minimalist High-Fidelity Grid Pattern

**Layout Philosophy & Visual Grid**
- Adopt a strict, ultra-clean geometric grid pattern reminiscent of elite gaming studio interfaces. Use extremely thin, low-opacity off-white (#f9f2e4) grid lines to frame content areas elegantly, echoing the layout lines of a traditional Mahjong table setup.
- Color balancing: Solid blocks of #143322 form the canvas. UI elements like badges, small utility icons, and pricing toggles utilize the bone off-white (#f9f2e4) texture. Rust (#b65a2f) is applied exclusively to active transactional buttons (Download, Subscribe) to ensure instant visual hierarchy.

**Interactive Interactions & Gamification Motion**
- **Section 1 (Hero Mosaic):** The right half of the viewport features a mathematical grid wall of 3D Mahjong tiles. The tile flip animation runs as a clean spiral from the center outward, revealing the sliced logo asset seamlessly. Maintain crisp, clean edge alignments with realistic soft drop shadows to give depth to the luxury tiles.
- **Section 3 & 4 (Subscription & Login):** Input fields and subscription cards should be minimalist blocks with thin off-white borders. When a card is hovered or focused, it performs a subtle 3D lift, and the border accents transition smoothly into a rich Rust (#b65a2f) color.
- **Section 5 (FAQ Clack Accordion):** Questions should be styled as long, horizontal row tiles. Clicking a row causes it to drop down with zero lag, deploying a snapping animation that feels identical to sliding a physical tile securely into place on a gaming deck.
